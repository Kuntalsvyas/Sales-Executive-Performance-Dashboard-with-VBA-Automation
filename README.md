## üìå Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end performance tracking system designed to evaluate Sales Executives across multiple regions. Moving beyond static reporting, I implemented **VBA/Macro automation** to standardize the data cleaning process and generate instant performance reports.
The tool provides management with a clear view of who is hitting targets, who is eligible for commissions, and where the sales bottlenecks are located.

## üõ†Ô∏è Technical Toolkit
- **VBA/Macros:** Automation of repetitive formatting and data preparation tasks.

## üìä Key Business Metrics (KPIs)
- **Achievement Index:** Tracking the % of target met by each Sales Executive.
- **Incentive Calculation:** Dynamic calculation of bonuses based on tiered performance levels.
- **Managerial Overview:** High-level view of team performance grouped by Senior Managers.
- **Top/Bottom Performers:** Automated ranking of the top 5 and bottom 5 executives to assist in HR reviews.
